## **Kicks Routine (60–75 minutes)**

##### Warm-ups:

* Dynamic stretches
* Controlled kicks (slow front, side, roundhouse chamber holds)
* 2-3 reps each leg

##### Isometric Kick Holds:

1 minute rest per set

* Side kick hold at hip height - 3 sets of 20 seconds per leg (5 minutes)
* Roundhouse chamber hold - 3 sets of 20 seconds per leg (5 minutes)

##### Plyometric Kicks:

2 minute rest per set

* Switch kicks - 3 sets of 30 seconds explosive tempo
* Jumping roundhouse kicks - 3 sets of 8 reps per leg
* Spinning back kicks - 3 sets of 6 reps each leg

#### Shadow boxing with kicks:

1 minute rest per set

* 2-3 punch combo finish with kick
* Include lateral footwork, pivots, stance changes
* 3 sets of 3 minutes per rep

#### Band work:

* Banded hip rotations - 2 sets of 12 reps each leg
* Banded rotational chops - 2 sets of 10 reps each leg
* Banded single-leg raisers - 2 sets of 20 second holds
